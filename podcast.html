Molecular Gastronomy

Welcome to the world of molecular gastronomy, a revolutionary field that has transformed the culinary landscape by applying scientific principles to cooking. Molecular gastronomy isn’t just about mixing chemicals with food; it’s a marriage of art and science that has given rise to new techniques, ingredients, and culinary experiences.

The Science of friction

In science, though, friction has a very specific meaning. Friction is the force felt between two surfaces when one attempts to slide against the other — whether or not they are moving. It always acts to slow things down. And it depends on only two things: the nature of the surfaces and how hard one presses against the other.
